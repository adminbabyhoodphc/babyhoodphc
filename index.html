About Us

At Babyhood we provide healthcare for children ranging in age from infancy to adolescence. Our practice's primary focus is your child's well-being. Kids require medical care curated for their unique needs—involving growth from start to finish—that is delivered by trained Child Specialist in environments designed just for your kids. As patients, children require

  • Medication dosing and equipment sized for kids
  • Extra time, more monitoring and effective communications
  • Compassionate caregivers who understand kids’ physical, mental and behavioral development.

Our vision is to give patient and their family a focused health care in a comprehensive environment that prompts viable treatment at affordable price. We are proud to stand with our feet strongly planted in our local community and as a household reachable clinic.

Services

We ensure the latest advances in pediatric-centered care is followed accurately as per Indian Medical Association & Indian Academy of Pediatrics. Below are just some of the pediatric medical services our practice provides.

Adolescent Medicine

We ensure focus is provided to kids who are in their early adolescent period of development. We answer all their questions on early changes and make them understand their puberty developments.

Infectious Disease Treatment

We identify and treat Infectious diseases that are caused by pathogenic microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi or parasites. Some common are lower respiratory infections, diarrheal diseases.

Growth & Development

We use some of the best criteria for the assessment of growth and nutritional status of child. We use various medical assessment & calibration to identify developmental delay if the child does not reach the expected developmental milestones for the age.

Vaccination

We use immunization for controlling and eliminating life-threatening infectious diseases. Vaccination stimulates your kids immune system so that it can recognise the disease and protect him/her from future infection.

Nutrition Councelling

We’ll review your kids medical history that includes medications, lifestyle, sleep patterns, energy levels, exercise, and gastrointestinal function and provide dietary preferences and cooking tips with regular household ingredients.

New Born Care

New borns are assessed for birth weight, head circumference, congenital defects and signs of newborn illness. Special care will be provided for sick newborns, those who are preterm and/or low birth weight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our pediatric services, doctors, or locations? Start with some of the most frequently asked questions.

  • What if my child isn't growing and attaining milestones at the right age?

    All children are different and grow at different paces. Some children mature in bursts, some at a steady pace. However, if you have questions about your child’s development, consult us for a Growth and Development assessment.

  • I have questions about my kids immunizations. Who should I talk to?

    Your pediatrician is your best resource. Be open about your questions. Your pediatrician knows you want what’s best for your child and respects your caution.

  • How often do my children need a medical checkup?

    After the first year, healthy children, without ongoing conditions or other issues, can typically get assessed once a year. However, this is only a basic rule. Questions or concerns about your child’s health? Feel free to consult with us any anytime.

  • Can i visit the clinic and meet the pediatrician before my baby is born?

    Yes! We encourage and welcome new parents to schedule a Prenatal visit. You’ll have a chance to get acquainted with us and our clinic, as well as ask any questions or medical doubts you have during your pre & post pregnancy.

  • Do you have both morning & evening weekend hours?

    Yes! We offer both morning & evening hours Monday through Saturday by appointments, direct walk-ins between 09:30 am to 12:00 pm & 05:30 pm to 09:30 pm respectively.

  • Where are is Babhyhood Premier Health Care situated?

    We are situated at Anna Nagar West Extension very close to Chennai Public School in TVS Avenue.
